- Download [dgVoodoo2](http://dege.freeweb.hu/dgVoodoo2/dgVoodoo2/#latest-stable-version)
- Download [D3DRM (Direct3d Retained mode)](http://dege.freeweb.hu/dgVoodoo2/bin/D3DRM.zip)
- Extract the dgVoodoo2 glide dlls into the oldeq folder.
- Extract the d3drm dll into the oldeq folder.
- Use compatibility settings to force 256 color.
- Run EverQuest.exe, once the game is rendered hit F2.
