A variation of the DirectX SDK [FastFile](/formats/fast_file) sample.

Magic `02 3d ff ff`

## Extensions

  - `.ff`
  - `.t3d`

## Names

- "FF", FastFile
- "WAD", reference to Doom's _Where's All the Data?_
- "t3dFastFile", True3d FastFile
